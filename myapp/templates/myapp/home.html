Aero Aid Drone Service

Aero Aid Drone Service is a pioneering initiative connected with the NGO sector and the Government of India, dedicated to enhancing disaster management efforts across the nation. We leverage advanced drone technology to provide efficient, real-time aerial support for various disaster response operations, including search and rescue missions, damage assessment, and logistical support.

Our services are designed to improve situational awareness, enabling rapid decision-making and effective resource allocation during emergencies. With a skilled team of trained professionals and a fleet of state-of-the-art drones, we are committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of communities across India. Together, we strive to mitigate the impact of disasters and enhance the resilience of our nation through innovation and collaboration.
